# Pricing & Bonus Systems: Precision Operations

This document details the monetization and incentive models for the MyTypist **Document Operating System**.

## 1. Metered Operations (Pricing Model)
Move beyond "SaaS Tiers" to **Metered Operations**. Users pay for document craftsmanship and operational throughput.

### Pricing Structure & Tiers
- **Identity**: Professional, Enterprise, Infrastructure.
- **Localization**: Retain v1 **Nigerian Naira (₦)** pricing as the baseline for the regional market.
- **Model**: Port v1 **Monthly/Quarterly toggle** logic with restrained, professional badges.

## 2. Interaction: The Pricing Calculator
- **Source**: v2 **Usage Calculator**.
- **UX Goal**: An operational tool to help teams estimate their **Document Throughput** and required token volume.
- **Design**: **Precision Editorial**. Monochrome surfaces, high-fidelity tables, and high-density information. No generic marketing icons.

## 3. Incentive Systems: The Bonus Monitor
- **Growth Logic**: Port v1 **Bonus System** logic (referrals, loyalty) but re-skin it as a "Professional Growth Monitor."
- **Dashboard Integration**: Bonuses must be visible as part of the operational identity in the spatial dashboard.
